# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** December 22, 2025

### 1. Introduction
**Nazarie Miron** respects the privacy of our users . This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our mobile application **Rise** (the "Application").

By using the Application, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

### 2. Collection of Your Information
We collect information that allows us to provide the "round-up" savings and financial tracking features of the Application. The types of information we collect include:

**A. Personal Data**
Personally identifiable information that you voluntarily give to us during registration:
* Name and Email address
* Phone number
* Date of Birth
* Location data (City, Country)

**B. Financial Data (via Plaid & Stripe)**
We do **not** store your bank account login credentials (username or password) on our servers.
* We use **Plaid** to connect to your financial institutions. When you connect a bank account, Plaid provides us with a secure token and access to your transaction data (e.g., transaction amounts, merchant names) to calculate "round-ups."
* We use **Stripe** for payment processing.

**C. Device Data**
Information our servers automatically collect when you access the Application, such as your mobile device ID (e.g., Apple IDFA or Android AdID), operating system version, and IP address.

### 3. Use of Your Information
We use the information we collect to:
* Create and manage your account.
* Calculate "round-up" amounts based on your transaction history.
* Process savings transfers via our payment processors.
* Verify your identity to comply with financial regulations (KYC/AML).
* Prevent fraudulent transactions and theft.
* Email you regarding your account or security updates.

### 4. Disclosure of Your Information
We share your information with specific third parties only when necessary to perform the services of the Application:

* **Google Firebase:** Used for secure authentication, database hosting (Firestore), and app analytics.
* **Plaid Inc.:** Used to connect your bank accounts. By using our app, you grant Plaid the right, power, and authority to act on your behalf to access and transmit your personal and financial information from your relevant financial institution. You agree to your personal and financial information being transferred, stored, and processed by Plaid in accordance with the [Plaid Privacy Policy](https://plaid.com/legal/).
* **Stripe:** Used to process money transfers. Data shared with Stripe is subject to the [Stripe Privacy Policy](https://stripe.com/privacy).

### 5. Data Security and Storage
We use administrative, technical, and physical security measures to help protect your personal information.
* Your data is stored securely using **Google Cloud Platform** (Firebase) infrastructure.
* Financial credentials are never accessible to us; they are encrypted and handled exclusively by Plaid and Stripe.

### 6. Data Retention and Deletion
We retain your personal information only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy.

**Your Right to Delete:**
You have the right to request the deletion of your account and all associated data. You can perform this action immediately within the Application by navigating to **Settings > Delete Account**.

Upon deletion, we will:
1.  Delete your user account from Firebase Authentication.
2.  Delete your personal data from our Firestore database.
3.  Request the deletion of your connected banking data from Plaid.

### 7. Children's Privacy
The Application is intended for users who are of legal age to form a binding contract (18+ in most jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

### 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date of this policy.
